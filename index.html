i used to always just stay on the menu cause the song was that good. i have grown to aprreciate city trial mode but i still like air ride and top ride better.

first traditional kirby game i played (first kirby game being kirby air ride). i remember i stayed up into the morning on a school break day just to 100% the game. i think it was 12/26/23? not sure.

sky force series

mario and luigi dream team

one of the first rpgs that i played for myself and with no one else's help. also my first 3ds game. my ds is actually limited edition (cool!).
